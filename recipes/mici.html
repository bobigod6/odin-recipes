Micii mici dar potriviti

Description

"Mici", it's a romanian nation dish made of ground beef miced with pork.

"Mici cu gust de mare mediteraneana" it's a reinterprated recipe by chief Sorin Scarlatescu Dumitrescu Catalin Florin Bontea.

My respects to the chef!

Ingredients

  • ground beef and pork

Steps

  1. shape then ""cum iti iese"
  2. grill them
  3. enjoy! \^_^/
